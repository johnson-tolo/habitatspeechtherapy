Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question not answered here? Reach out directly - we're happy to talk.

What are the benefits of having speech therapy at home?

Learning occurs in the child's natural environment, which means the skills they build carry over more easily into real life. Your child can practice with their own familiar toys, games, and daily routines - the contexts they already know and love. There's also no commute, which reduces stress for the whole family and means therapy fits more naturally into your day.

For young children especially, being in a familiar space with familiar people makes it easier to engage and take communication risks - the foundation of meaningful progress.

What services do you offer?

Habitat Speech Therapy provides speech and language therapy and evaluations in the home, as well as in participating private schools and daycare programs if needed. Services include:

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple. Reach out by email, text, or through the contact form on our Contact page to set up an initial call. We'll have a warm, personal conversation about your child's needs and talk through next steps together - no pressure, no commitment required to just talk.

What is PROMPT, and does my child need it?

PROMPT (Prompts for Restructuring Oral Muscular Phonetic Targets) is a specialized, evidence-based approach for treating childhood apraxia of speech and complex articulation disorders. It uses tactile-kinesthetic cues - gentle touch on the face and jaw - to help children feel how sounds are made, not just hear them.

Athena holds a PROMPT Level 1 certification. Whether PROMPT is appropriate for your child depends on their specific diagnosis and needs, which we'd assess during an evaluation.
