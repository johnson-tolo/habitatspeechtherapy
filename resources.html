Resources for Families

Trusted information to help you understand your child's development and know when to seek support.

Communication Milestones

Understanding what's typical at each age is the first step in knowing whether your child may benefit from an evaluation.

  • ASHA Communication Milestones (Birth – Age 5)
  • CDC Developmental Milestones (2 Months – 5 Years)

Identifying Concerns Early

Early identification makes a real difference. These tools can help you recognize signs that a child may benefit from a speech or language evaluation.

  • ASHA: Speech & Language Disorders in Children
  • Identify the Signs - Recognizing Communication Disorders

Screen Time & Technology

How technology is used - and how much - can shape language development in young children. These guides offer evidence-based recommendations for families.

  • Be Tech Wise With Baby (PDF) - ASHA
  • Be Tech Wise With Toddler (PDF) - ASHA

Have a Question?

Resources are a starting point - but every child is different. If something feels off, trust your instincts and reach out. A conversation costs nothing and can make all the difference.
