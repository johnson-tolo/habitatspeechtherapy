About Us

A little about the person behind Habitat Speech Therapy

Hi, I'm Athena

I'm a licensed speech-language pathologist with over a decade of clinical experience working with children across New York, California, and Florida. My journey has taken me through Pre-K through 12th grade settings, early intervention programs throughout Southern California, and now into the homes of families across Palm Beach County.

I founded Habitat Speech Therapy because I believe deeply in meeting children where they are - literally. In-home therapy allows me to observe how your child communicates in their real world, and to build therapy into the routines and activities that already fill their day.

Beyond clinical skill, my background in Human Factors - the science of designing learning experiences that are accessible and effective - shapes every session I plan. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. I build approaches that work for your specific child, in your specific home.
