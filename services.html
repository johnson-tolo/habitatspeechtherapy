Our Services

Personalized, in-home speech and language therapy — and evaluations — for children of all ages. Services are available in the home, as well as in participating private schools and daycare programs.

🔍

All Ages

Speech & Language Evaluations

Before therapy begins, a thorough evaluation helps us understand exactly where your child is — and where they're headed. Our evaluations are comprehensive, taking a holistic view of your child's individual communication strengths and needs rather than simply generating a number or a label.

The results of an evaluation guide everything: whether therapy is recommended, what goals to prioritize, and how progress will be measured. You'll come away with a clear picture and a path forward.

🌱

Ages 18 months – 3 years

Early Intervention Toddler Speech Therapy

The first three years of life are a critical window for language development. Early intervention services support young children with developmental delays or disabilities, helping them build foundational communication skills during the period when the brain is most receptive to learning.

Sessions are play-based and naturalistic, woven into the routines and activities your toddler already loves. In-home delivery is especially powerful at this age — your child is at their most comfortable, and we can collaborate with caregivers in real time.

🗣️

All Ages

Articulation & Speech Sound Therapy

If your child is difficult to understand — or struggles with specific sounds — articulation therapy can make a meaningful difference. We target the clarity of spoken language for children with speech delays or speech sound disorders, using evidence-based techniques including PROMPT (Prompts for Restructuring Oral Muscular Phonetic Targets) for children with apraxia of speech or complex articulation challenges.

The goal is clear, confident communication — at home, at school, and everywhere in between.

💬

All Ages

Receptive & Expressive Language Therapy

Language has two sides, and we address both.

Receptive language — understanding — includes skills like comprehending questions, following multi-step directions, and processing what others say.

Expressive language — producing — includes skills like using age-appropriate vocabulary, forming grammatically correct sentences, using correct verb tenses, and telling stories.

Sessions are tailored to your child's specific profile, targeting the areas that make the biggest difference in their daily life.

🤝

All Ages

Social Communication

Pragmatic language is the social side of communication — knowing not just what to say, but how, when, and with whom. Social communication therapy targets the skills that help children connect with peers and navigate the social world: taking turns in conversation, reading non-verbal cues, adjusting language for different listeners, and understanding the unspoken rules of interaction.

These skills are practiced in naturalistic settings, making in-home therapy a particularly effective format for this type of work.
